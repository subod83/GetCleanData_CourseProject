## Code Book



The measurement variables used are specified in camel case. The parts of the variable names are explained below



###subject 
    identifier of the subject

###activity
    activity performed by the subject

###time
    measurement of time

###frequency
    measurement of frequency

###Freq 
    frequency

###Body
    Body

###Acc
    Acceleration

###Jerk
    Jerk signal

###Gravity
    Gravity

###Gyro
    Gyroscope

###Mag
    Magnitude

###angle
    angular momentum

###X 
    along the X axis

###Y
    along the Y axis

###Z  
    along the Z axis

###mean 
    mean value
 
###std 
    standard deviation
